About CSD

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering (Data Science) was established in 2022 with an intake of 60 students. The program aims to meet the growing demand for skilled professionals in data science and artificial intelligence. It provides a strong foundation in computer science, statistics, and optimization, enabling students to extract meaningful insights from data.

The curriculum blends data inference, algorithm development, and technology to solve complex analytical problems. Students develop expertise in mathematics, technology, and business strategy to leverage data creatively and drive business value.
